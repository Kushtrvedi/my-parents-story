# Privacy Policy

**My Parents' Story**
Last updated: July 2026

## Overview

My Parents' Story is a free, offline-first mobile application that helps families preserve their parents' life stories through voice recording and guided conversation. We built this app with one principle above all else: **your memories belong to you, not us.**

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

My Parents' Story operates entirely on your device. No data is transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

### What We Store Locally

All data stays on your device:

- **Voice recordings** — audio of stories you choose to record
- **Transcriptions** — text converted from your recordings
- **Memoirs** — edited story text generated from your recordings
- **Profile information** — names, birth years, and cities you enter
- **Photos** — images you attach to memories (if you choose)
- **App preferences** — language selection and settings

### What We Never Collect

- We do not collect personal information
- We do not track your location
- We do not use analytics or tracking services
- We do not use advertising
- We do not collect device identifiers
- We do not collect usage statistics

## Voice Recording

The app uses your device's microphone to record stories. Audio recordings are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted anywhere. The recording feature requires your explicit permission each time you record.

## Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| **Microphone** | To record voice stories. Only used when you tap the record button. |
| **Storage** (if applicable) | To save PDF files and photos you choose to export. |

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, security depends on your device's built-in protections. We recommend:

- Using a screen lock on your device
- Keeping your device's operating system updated
- Backing up your data regularly using the app's export feature

## Data Export and Backup

You can export your stories as PDF files and share them with family members. You can also back up your data to your device's local storage. You are always in control of your data.

## Children's Privacy

This app is designed for use by families. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or age verification.

## Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. There are no SDKs that transmit data externally.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will notify you through the app. Any changes will only apply to data collected after the update takes effect.
