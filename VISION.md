# Vision

We are not building a journaling app.

We are preserving the voices, stories, and wisdom of parents before they are lost.

Every feature must strengthen that purpose.

---

## The Problem

Parents carry entire libraries inside them.

First jobs. Wedding days. Lessons learned the hard way. Advice that shaped a family. Stories that connect generations.

Most of those stories are never written down.

When a parent passes, those stories go with them.

There is no undo button for an unwritten legacy.

---

## The Solution

A simple, beautiful app that helps families capture their parents' stories through guided conversation.

No accounts required. No cloud dependency. No complexity.

Just a patient interface that listens while a parent speaks, and transforms those spoken memories into a lasting memoir.

---

## What We Believe

- Every parent deserves to leave behind their story
- Memories should belong to families, not companies
- The best technology disappears — it just works
- Simplicity is not a limitation, it is a discipline
- Legacy matters more than features

---

## What We Are Not

- Not a productivity app
- Not a journal
- Not an AI companion
- Not a social network
- Not a data harvesting operation

---

## Success Looks Like

A grandchild opens the app thirty years from now.

They hear their grandfather's voice telling the story of his first day at work.

They read his words about what love means.

They understand, for the first time, the full story of the person who shaped their family.

That is the product we are building.
