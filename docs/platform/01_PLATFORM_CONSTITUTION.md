# Platform Constitution

**My Parents' Story is not an app. It is an oral-history platform.**

The goal of this platform is to preserve family knowledge—conversations, relationships, traditions, and life events—as enduring, structured artifacts. AI is not the product; AI is the interface that makes collecting those stories feel natural, empathetic, and human.

---

## 1. The Single Source of Truth
The **Life Graph** is the canonical data model and the single source of truth for the platform.

**Rule 1: Decoupling Input from Output**
No product, feature, or export format (Memoir, Family Tree, Cookbook) depends directly on the conversation.
- The Conversation Engine writes to the Life Graph.
- All Future Products read from the Life Graph.
- The conversation is simply one interface for collecting data.

## 2. Source Attribution
Every single fact stored in the Life Graph must know where it came from.
- **Traceability:** A `Person` or `Event` must hold a reference to the exact story, audio segment, and timestamp where they were mentioned.
- **Why?** This ensures absolute trust. Years later, a family member can read a sentence in a generated biography and immediately listen to the original 15-second audio clip of their parent saying it.

## 3. Separation of Facts vs. Interpretations
The platform strictly separates immutable facts from subjective AI interpretations.

- **Facts:** "Born in 1952", "Lived in Indore", "Married in 1978". Facts are ground truth. They do not change unless manually corrected by the family.
- **Interpretations:** "Courageous", "Sacrifice", "Family-first", "Optimistic". Interpretations are generated by the Legacy Intelligence Layer. They may evolve, be recalculated, or shift as more stories are collected.

## 4. The Legacy Composer
The platform no longer generates a "book" directly from an interview. Instead, a **Legacy Composer** queries the Life Graph to produce diverse outputs:
- Memoir
- Cookbook
- Family Timeline
- Biography
- Tribute Speech
- Anniversary Booklet
- Museum-style Archive
- "Ask Grandpa" Voice Interface

## 5. Architectural Stability
From this point forward, the core platform architecture is frozen.
- New features should extend the Life Graph.
- Existing entities should evolve through versioning.
- Engineering effort focuses on delivering tangible product value to families, not refactoring the AI infrastructure.
