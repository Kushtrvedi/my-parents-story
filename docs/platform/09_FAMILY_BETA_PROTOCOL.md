# 09. Family Beta Protocol

This protocol defines how to evaluate the Family Beta. The focus at this stage is exclusively on observing how real families use the product and gathering qualitative, emotional feedback.

## 1. Beta Constraint Policy
During the Family Beta, the following rules apply to development:

**FROZEN:**
*   Life Graph
*   Conversation Engine
*   Timeline Engine
*   Legacy Intelligence

**ALLOWED FIXES:**
*   Bugs
*   Performance optimizations
*   Reliability improvements
*   Accessibility enhancements

**No new AI features or structural changes are to be introduced during the Beta.** This discipline ensures clean feedback from real users.

---

## 2. Session Capture

For every tester, capture the following technical baseline:

*   **Device:**
*   **Browser:**
*   **Operating System:**
*   **Internet Connection:**
*   **PWA Installed:** (Yes / No)

### 2.1 Behavioral Metrics
*   Time to first recording.
*   Time to first completed memory.
*   Time to first Legacy Book generation.
*   Observe any confusion points.
*   Observe any technical issues or stutters.

---

## 3. Emotional Feedback Questionnaire

Do not ask generic questions like "Did you like it?". Instead, use the following questions to uncover the emotional impact of the product:

1.  *Was there a moment that felt particularly meaningful?*
2.  *Did any question surprise you?*
3.  *Did you feel like you were being interviewed or listened to?*
4.  *Would you recommend this to another family?*
5.  *Would you continue using it after today?*

These answers will shape the product far more than another architectural improvement.

---

## 4. Success Metrics

Family Beta success is defined by outcomes, not technical metrics or download counts.

### 4.1 Technical Success
*   Zero crashes.
*   Zero data loss.
*   Zero routing failures (especially on deep links).
*   Reliable speech capture.
*   Successful Google Drive backups.
*   Legacy Book generation succeeds.

### 4.2 Product Success
*   Users complete their first story.
*   Users voluntarily record a second story.
*   Users finish at least one chapter.
*   Families share a generated Legacy Book.
*   Users return to the application on a different day.

### 4.3 Emotional Success (The Ultimate Indicator)
*   **Did the conversation help someone remember something they had forgotten?** *(If this happens consistently, the platform is succeeding.)*

---

## 5. Post-Beta Roadmap

If the Family Beta is successful based on the metrics above, the product will move forward in this order:

1.  **Family Beta Evaluation**
2.  **Polish the Heritage Linen Legacy Book**
3.  **Public Launch**
4.  **Printed Hardcover Edition**
5.  **Family Collaboration**
6.  **Life Graph Explorer**
7.  **Multi-generation Family Archives**

*Notice that every step adds value to families rather than expanding infrastructure. The platform architecture is complete.*
