# The Memoir Experience

With the core engineering platform and deployment pipelines stable, the ultimate focus of My Parents' Story shifts to the **Quality of the Final Artifact**.

This is the most critical phase of the product lifecycle. The first time someone opens the finished memoir, they must think:
> *"I can't believe this came from conversations with my parent."*

---

## 1. Vision: A Family Heirloom
We no longer use the term **"PDF Export"**. Language shapes expectations.
Instead, we:
- Create a Legacy Book
- Generate a Family Book
- Build a Memoir
- Prepare a Hardcover Edition

The output must not look like an exported PDF. It must feel like a premium coffee-table book that someone would proudly keep on a bookshelf for decades.

## 2. Book Designer & Legacy Composer
The architecture moves from a direct "PDF Export" to a multi-stage **Legacy Composer**:
`Life Graph -> Legacy Composer -> Book Designer -> Print Preview -> Hardcover PDF`

This decouples the visual layout from the Life Graph, allowing the same stories to power multiple editions.

## 3. Print-Ready Output Requirements
Future iterations of the Book Designer will target professional print-ready standards using web-native paging technologies (e.g., HTML/CSS via Paged.js).
Requirements include:
- Bleed and Safe Margins
- CMYK Print-Ready Workflow
- Embedded Fonts
- High-Resolution Images
- Dynamic Page Numbering
- Table of Contents
- Dedication and Signature Pages
- Family Tree Integration

## 4. Multi-Modal Experiences (Voice Integration)
The physical book should bridge the gap between text and audio.
Every chapter will optionally feature a **QR Code** that, when scanned by a grandchild twenty years from now, instantly plays the original audio recording of that specific memory.

## 5. Layout Themes
The Book Designer will eventually support distinct design systems:
1. **Classic Hardcover:** Timeless, academic serif typography.
2. **Minimal Scandinavian:** Clean, sans-serif, expansive whitespace.
3. **Vintage Family Album:** Warm tones, textured backgrounds, polaroid-style image treatments.
4. **Elegant Black & Gold:** Premium foil-style accents, dramatic contrast.
5. **Heritage Linen Edition:** Soft, fabric-inspired textures and elegant spacing.
