# Play Store Screenshot Story

## Brand: My Parents' Story
## Positioning: Preserve the voice, memories, and wisdom of the people you love.

---

## Narrative Structure

The five screenshots tell one emotional story: from realizing stories matter, to sitting with a parent, to hearing their voice, to holding a finished memoir, to passing it on.

Each screenshot shows the actual app UI. Minimal overlay text. The app does the talking.

---

## Screen 1: The Invitation

**Title**: The Invitation
**Headline**: Every family has stories worth preserving.
**Caption**: Start the conversation that matters most.

**App Screen**: Landing screen with warm welcome message.

**Visual Composition**:
- Show the full landing screen
- Warm cream background (#FAF8F5)
- Book icon centered
- "Every family has stories that deserve to be remembered" visible
- "Let's begin" button prominent
- Language selector visible (shows 12-language support)
- No people in this shot — just the app, clean and inviting

**Mood**: Quiet. Inviting. Like opening a door to something meaningful.

## Screen 2: The Question

**Title**: The Question
**Headline**: Ask the questions you've always wanted to ask.
**Caption**: 300 thoughtful prompts across 20 life chapters.

**App Screen**: Question screen showing a life chapter question.

**Visual Composition**:
- Show the question screen with a specific question visible

- Chapter name visible at top (e.g., "Childhood" or "Family Life")

- Recording button large and inviting

**Mood**: Intimate. Like sitting across from someone you love and finally asking.

## Overlay Text Guidelines

- Maximum 8 words per overlay
- White text with subtle shadow for readability
- Positioned at top or bottom of screen
- Never覆盖 core UI elements
- Font: Clean sans-serif (matches app typography)

## Color Treatment

- Screenshots use the app's actual color palette
- No added filters or effects
- Warm cream backgrounds (#FAF8F5)
- Forest green accents (#3A5A40)
- Gold highlights (#D4A373)
