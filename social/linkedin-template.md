# LinkedIn Update Template

**Hook (1 sentence):** Share a specific challenge or realization.

**The Story (3-4 short paragraphs):** 
- What were we trying to do?
- What went wrong or what did we discover?
- How did we fix it?

**The Result (1 sentence):** The tangible outcome.

**Call to Action:** Ask a genuine question to the community.

**Media:** Always attach a screenshot, diagram, or photo.
