# MARKETING REPORT

**Date**: 2026-07-21
**Status**: Final positioning locked

---

## Final Positioning

**One line**: Preserve the voice, memories, and wisdom of the people you love.

**What it is**: A guided conversation tool that helps you record your parents' life stories in their own voice and turn them into a beautiful memoir.

**What it is not**: An AI memoir generator. A voice recorder. A note-taking app. A social platform.

---

## Messaging Guidelines

### Primary Message
"Preserve the voice, memories, and wisdom of the people you love."

### Secondary Messages
- "Every family has stories that deserve to be remembered."
- "Ask the questions you've always wanted to ask."
- "Record their voice while you still can."
- "A gift your family will treasure forever."

### Tone of Voice
- **Warm** — like talking to someone you love
- **Patient** — never rushed, never pushy
- **Honest** — no hyperbole, no false promises
- **Respectful** — of the parent, the stories, and the family
- **Quiet** — let the content speak, not the marketing

### Words to Use
preserve, voice, memories, wisdom, family, stories, legacy, together, gift, trust, private, offline, free, beautiful, keepsake

### Words to Avoid
AI, machine learning, algorithm, cloud, data, analytics, sync, social, share, viral, growth, engagement, retention, monetization, subscription, premium, pro

## Launch Recommendations

### Before Closed Beta
1. Create 5 screenshots following the story specification
2. Generate 512x512 Play Store icon
3. Generate 1024x500 feature graphic
4. Build and test release APK on 3+ Android devices
5. Recruit 10-20 families for closed beta

### Closed Beta Strategy
- Target: Parents, grandparents, adult children
- Method: Watch them use the app without explanation
- Observe: Where they hesitate, what confuses them, what moves them
- Iterate: Fix friction points, preserve emotional moments

### After Beta Feedback
- Refine onboarding flow based on real usage
- Adjust question prompts if certain chapters underperform
- Consider Legacy Invitation as v1.1 feature
- Generate signed AAB and submit to Google Play
